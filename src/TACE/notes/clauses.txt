
Types of clauses.
Both sets of clauses WO and US
are partitionned acc. to the following categories.

- STDO = (t\leq)
- CSTE = (d) avec equalities
- CSTD = (d+) without equalities
  obtained from (d) by eq. elim.
- GOAL
- UNIT (without splitting literals) [opt]
- SPLIT- with neg. splitting literal
- SPLIT+ with pos. splitting literal
